Hi! I'm Gabriele James

I am a writer, researcher, and recipe-developer. Recently I started dabbling in web development, too.

In 2018 I graduted with a Masters degree in Philosophy from The University of Cape Town (UCT). My research and teaching focusses on various issues in applied ethics. During my graduate studies my husband and I opened an artisnal home bakery, specialising in vegan doughnuts. The business now focusses on recipe development and content-creation in the food space.

Apart from all that, I am a web-developer in training, currently completing an immersive full-stack web development bootcamp through HyperionDev.
